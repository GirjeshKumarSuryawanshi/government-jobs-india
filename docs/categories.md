# Recruitment Categories

GovtJobNow organizes recruitment information into multiple categories.

## SSC Jobs

Information related to Staff Selection Commission recruitment.

---

## UPSC Jobs

Recruitment information published by UPSC.

---

## Railway Jobs

Recruitment released by Railway Recruitment Boards.

---

## Banking Jobs

Recruitment from public sector banks and banking examination authorities.

---

## Defence Jobs

Recruitment for Army, Navy, Air Force, Coast Guard and related organizations.

---

## Police Jobs

Recruitment by police departments and state police organizations.

---

## Teaching Jobs

Recruitment from schools, universities and educational institutions.

---

## Engineering Jobs

Engineering opportunities across central and state government organizations.

---

## Medical Jobs

Healthcare and medical recruitment opportunities.

---

## PSU Recruitment

Public Sector Undertaking recruitment.

---

## 10th Pass Jobs

Recruitment opportunities requiring Class 10 qualification.

---

## 12th Pass Jobs

Recruitment opportunities requiring Class 12 qualification.

---

## Graduate Jobs

Recruitment for bachelor's degree holders.

---

## Diploma Jobs

Recruitment requiring diploma qualifications.

---

## ITI Jobs
