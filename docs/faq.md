# Frequently Asked Questions

## What is GovtJobNow?

GovtJobNow is an educational platform that publishes information related to Government Jobs, Recruitment Notifications, Admit Cards, Results, Syllabus, and other career resources.

---

## Is GovtJobNow a Government Website?

No.

GovtJobNow is an independent educational website.

---

## Does GovtJobNow conduct examinations?

No.

We only publish publicly available recruitment information.

---

## How often is the website updated?

New recruitment information is published whenever official notifications become available.

---

## Where does GovtJobNow collect information?

Information is collected from official recruitment notifications and public announcements released by recruiting authorities.

---

## Can I apply directly through GovtJobNow?

GovtJobNow provides official application links whenever available.

Applications are submitted on the respective official websites.
